Skill Upskill Reskill

Development of skills is the need of the hour. If we do not respond to this challenge, we face the prospect of becoming irrelevant. If, as skill professionals, we assume that our old time-served competences will last a lifetime, we will find ourselves becoming candidates for redundancy. The organisations we work for equally run the risk of failing to provide the new products and services that the market requires, resulting in decline. And so, we need to learn continually as we work. This requires a skill set that we need to learn for ourselves as teachers and mentors, and a skill set we need to instil into our learners for their future benefit.Hence having and developing skills is important.


Mahatma Gandhi

NREGA

It aims to enhance livelihood security in rural areas by providing at least 100 days of wage employment in a financial year to every household whose adult members volunteer to do unskilled manual work. One of the objectives is to improve the bargaining power of labour who often face exploitative market conditions. Several studies have found that agricultural wages have increased significantly, especially for women.

Pradhan Mantri

Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana

It is available to people between 18 and 50 years of age with bank accounts and the amount will be automatically debited from the account. In case of death due to any cause, the payment to the nominee will be ₹2 lakh. One of the objectives of PMJJBY is that all bank account holders can avail this facility through their net-banking service facility or filling a form at the bank branch at any time of the year.

Pradhan Mantri

Shram Yogi Maandhan

It was launched by the Ministry of Labour and Employment for poor labourers in the unorganised sector from minimum 18 years of age to maximum 40 years. To avail the scheme, the concerned person must visit the Community Service Centre. One of the objectives of this scheme is to ensure social welfare of migrant workers. Furthermore, the spouse of the registered person spouse has the option to continue the scheme.
